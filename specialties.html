Critical Care Nursing

Critical care nurses work in intensive care units (ICU) providing specialized care for critically ill patients. They monitor life support equipment, administer medications, and collaborate with healthcare teams.

Pediatric Nursing

Pediatric nurses specialize in caring for infants, children, and adolescents. They provide age-appropriate care, support families, and work in hospitals, clinics, and community health settings.

Watch More Videos

Perioperative Nursing

Perioperative nurses assist during surgical procedures, ensuring patient safety before, during, and after surgery. They work in operating rooms and manage sterile environments.

Watch More Videos

Oncology Nursing

Oncology nurses care for cancer patients, administering chemotherapy, managing symptoms, and providing emotional support to patients and their families throughout treatment.

Watch More Videos

Mental Health Nursing

Psychiatric nurses care for patients with mental health conditions, providing therapy, administering medications, and supporting recovery in hospitals and community settings.

Watch More Videos

Emergency Nursing

Emergency nurses work in ERs providing rapid assessment and treatment for trauma and acute illness. They thrive in fast-paced environments and make critical decisions under pressure.

Watch More Videos

Midwifery

Midwives provide care during pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum. They support natural birth processes and provide comprehensive reproductive health services.

Watch More Videos

Community Health Nursing

Community health nurses work in public health settings, promoting wellness, preventing disease, and providing care to underserved populations in communities.

Watch More Videos

Nephrology Nursing

Nephrology nurses care for patients with kidney disease, managing dialysis treatments and supporting patients with chronic kidney conditions.

Watch More Videos

Orthopedic Nursing

Orthopedic nurses care for patients with musculoskeletal conditions, assisting with joint replacements, fracture care, and rehabilitation after injuries.
